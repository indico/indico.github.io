Organize your events

Indico is the product of more than 10 years of development focused on delivering a versatile and powerful event management system. It is essential in the daily life of several research communities and organisations alike.

Event organisation workflow that fits lectures, meetings, workshops and conferences.

Easy upload and retrieval of presentations, papers and other documents.

Multi-granular tree-based protection scheme

Conference paper reviewing functionalities.

Room booking and management

Full coverage of the conference life cicle.

Integration with several collaborative tools

Permanent archival of all event material and metadata.

Indico is Open Source software. It's free to use and easy to modify!
